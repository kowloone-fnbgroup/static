Account Deletion & Data Removal

This page explains how users of KowlooneApp (by Kowloone Group) can delete their account and what happens to their data afterward.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Open the KowlooneApp app.
  2. Go to Profile > Edit Profile > Delete Account.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm.
  4. Your account will be deactivated immediately after you confirmed.

Can’t access the app? Email us: kowloone.fnbgroup.tech@gmail.com

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Profile information (e.g., name, email, phone)
  • Login credentials and authentication data
  • App activity history (e.g., reservations, orders, loyalty points)
  • User‑generated content (e.g., reviews, comments, uploads)

Data That May Be Retained

  • Transaction/payment records for 5 years (legal/tax compliance)
  • Fraud prevention & security logs for 3 months
  • Server backups for up to 2 weeks (then auto‑purged)

Retained data is stored securely and not used for marketing.

Retention Periods

  • Deleted immediately: profile, login details, preferences, app activity history.
  • Temporary backups: retained up to 3 days before automatic removal.
  • Required by law: transaction/payment data for up to 5 years.

Notes

Important:

  • Once deleted, your account and data cannot be recovered.
